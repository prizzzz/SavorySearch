Frequently Asked Questions

How does SavorySearch work?

Simply enter the ingredients you have, and our algorithm will find recipes that use those ingredients. You can filter by diet, cuisine, or cooking time.

Is SavorySearch free to use?

Yes! Our basic service is completely free. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Absolutely! Just click the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your favorites for easy access later.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to search for recipes. However, creating an account allows you to save favorites across devices.
